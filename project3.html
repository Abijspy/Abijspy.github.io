Smart Water Supply and Management System

A smart water management system basically refers to a system created to collect information on the distribution, pressure, and flow of water in a city or town. Our principal objective is to improve the current system for managing and meeting the water needs of urban areas

● The main source for water is the Dam water or the River water. Here, the water is purified and identifies the diseases in the water. After the disease identification the water is sent to the tank and this tank water is sent to the various houses nearby through a pipeline.

● A particular room is kept for identifying the leakage detection in the pipes, reach of water through the destination, and whether anyone is extracting water from the tank illegally. The water logging in some areas is also extracted and purified and it is used.

● After the usage of water in every house, the waste water from the kitchen, bathroom etc, this water is purified and stored in the recycled water tank in each house. Which can be used for other purposes like gardening etc. Similarly, every house and the main tank has a rainwater harvesting system which is purified and stored in the sump which can be used later and in water scarcity periods.

Our Project divided into different parts

  • Water Level Indication
  • Checking Purity of Water
  • Reach of Water to the Destination
  • Leakage Detection
  • Rainwater Harvesting
  • Recycling of Water

We have developed a method to regulate the elements of the supply system, including water pressure, water quality, and water pollution.This system can be used in smart cities, towns, villages, and other locations where intelligent and sophisticated water management and distribution is required.
